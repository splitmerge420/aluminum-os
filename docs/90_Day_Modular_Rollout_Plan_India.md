    # 📄 ARTIFACT 1: 90‑Day Modular Rollout Plan  
    ## First 100 DISH‑Printed Bio‑CNG Nodes (India)
    
    **Purpose:** Accelerate the Banas model from 18‑month construction to **90‑day deployment** using distributed manufacturing (DISH‑printing) and pre‑approved templates.
    
    ---
    
    ### 1. Site Selection (0–15 days)
    
    | Criterion | Details | Priority Zones |
    |-----------|---------|----------------|
    | **High stubble burning** | >50,000 hectares of paddy/wheat burning annually | Punjab (Amritsar, Ludhiana), Haryana (Karnal, Kurukshetra), Western UP |
    | **Existing cooperative base** | Dairy or farmer cooperative with >500 members | Milk unions, FPOs, GOBARdhan units |
    | **Logistics** | Within 20‑25 km of 400+ cattle; access to NH/state highway | Clusters with existing CBG plants (e.g., Banas model in Gujarat) |
    | **Land availability** | 2‑5 acres, non‑agricultural, with water access | Land already identified under GOBARdhan scheme |
    
    **Deliverable:** List of 100 sites, mapped with coordinates, cooperative partner, and local government approval status.
    
    ---
    
    ### 2. Standardized Node Spec (DISH‑Printed)
    
    | Component | Source | Notes |
    |-----------|--------|-------|
    | **Digester vessels** | DISH‑printed in India (IIT Madras + Tsinghua template) | 100 TPD capacity; 90‑day print time per vessel |
    | **Gas cleaning / compression** | Pre‑fab modules from Chinese / Indian suppliers | Standardized SATAT‑compliant unit |
    | **FOM processing** | Local fabrication (pug mills, drying beds) | Designed for low‑tech operation |
    | **Sensor suite** | Open‑source hardware (Arduino / ESP32) + AI firmware | Monitors pressure, temperature, gas quality; data logged to Optical Ledger |
    | **Power / grid connection** | Diesel generator backup; net‑metering ready | For biogas engines; surplus power fed to grid |
    
    **Total capital cost per node:** ₹50–55 crore (similar to Banas), but DISH‑printing reduces construction time from 18 months to 90 days.
    
    ---
    
    ### 3. Implementation Timeline (90 Days)
    
    | Phase | Duration | Activities | Responsible |
    |-------|----------|------------|-------------|
    | **0–15 days** | 2 weeks | Site selection, land lease, cooperative agreements, permits | State nodal agency + GOBARdhan |
    | **15–45 days** | 4 weeks | DISH‑printing of digester vessels (parallel across 10–20 printing hubs) | IIT Madras + Chinese partner (Tsinghua) |
    | **30–60 days** | 4 weeks | Site prep (foundations, piping, electrical), installation of pre‑fab modules | Local contractors + IIT Madras supervision |
    | **45–75 days** | 4 weeks | Sensor installation, AI coordination setup, staff training | Pantheon AI (GangaSeek) + local IT partners |
    | **75–90 days** | 2 weeks | Commissioning, testing, first CBG output, certification | GOBARdhan + independent auditor |
    
    **Key enabler:** Pre‑approved “building permit in a box” for all 100 sites, coordinated by the Union Ministry of Cooperation.
    
    ---
    
    ### 4. Funding & Incentives
    
    | Source | Amount (₹ crore) | Mechanism |
    |--------|------------------|-----------|
    | **IBA proposed fund** | ₹10,000 | Capital subsidy per node: ₹6 crore (increased from ₹4 crore) |
    | **Tier 2 Infrastructure Bonds** | $10‑20B (₹80‑160k crore) | Bonds repaid via node revenues; issued through BRICS New Development Bank |
    | **Carbon credits** | ~₹2‑5 crore/year per node | Monetized via domestic market; can be front‑loaded as financing |
    | **State government incentives** | Varies | Gujarat model: ₹60 crore allocated for 10 nodes |
    
    **Total funding needed for 100 nodes:** ~₹5,500 crore (₹55 crore × 100). Recoverable within 4–5 years via CBG/FOM sales.
    
    ---
    
    ### 5. Verification & Governance (GangaSeek)
    
    - **Optical Ledger** records each node’s construction milestones, ownership, and operational data.
    - **Open Auditability:** Sensor data aggregated and anonymized; available to GOBARdhan and Pentagonal Council for verification.
    - **Dharma invariant:** Regional Veto Shard ensures no node diverts water/resources from marginalized communities.
    - **Worker equity:** 51% of each node owned by the local cooperative.
    
    ---
    
    ### 6. Expected Impact (First 100 Nodes)
    
    | Metric | Value |
    |--------|-------|
    | **CBG production** | ~180 tonnes/day → 65,700 tonnes/year → replaces ~2% of LPG imports from Hormuz |
    | **FOM production** | ~10,000 tonnes/day → 3.65M tonnes/year → replaces ~15‑20% of current DAP/urea imports for surrounding farms |
    | **Stubble burning reduction** | ~1M tonnes of paddy straw diverted → significant reduction in Delhi winter smog |
    | **Farmers benefitted** | 40,000‑50,000 families (400‑500 per node) |
    | **CO₂e avoided** | ~675,000 tonnes/year (from methane capture and avoided burning) |

    ### 7. Next Steps
    
    1. **Formalize partnership** with IIT Madras and Tsinghua to finalize DISH‑printed digester design.
    2. **Secure pre‑approval** from Ministry of New & Renewable Energy for the 100 sites.
    3. **Launch first 10 nodes** within 90 days as proof‑of‑concept; scale to 100 by end of 2026.
