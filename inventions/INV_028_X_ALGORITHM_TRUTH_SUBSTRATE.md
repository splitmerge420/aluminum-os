## Proposed INV-28: Information Integrity Transparency

When Aluminum OS processes external information, integrity metadata SHALL be computed and made available. Includes: incentive distortion (MED-9), source authority, multi-model consensus, temporal freshness.

Integrity metadata SHALL NOT be used for: user profiling, advertiser sharing, law enforcement surveillance, binary censorship.

Demotion SHALL be: proportional (never binary), transparent (users inspect), appealable, auditable (INV-27 PQC-signed).
