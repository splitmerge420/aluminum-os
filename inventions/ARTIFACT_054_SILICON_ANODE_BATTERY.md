# Artifact 54: Silicon Anode Battery

## Invention Details
**Inventor:** Dave Sheldon  
**Vaulted:** March 11, 2026  
**Composition:** C13/Si28 Sheldonium composite spinoff

## Technical Overview

Si28 isotopic purity reduces phonon scattering improving structural uniformity, potentially reducing cracking failure mode. C13 matrix accommodates silicon volume expansion. 50-50 ratio may provide buffering structure that pure silicon anodes lack.

### Performance Characteristics
- **Energy Density:** 10x higher theoretical energy density than graphite anodes
- **Composition Ratio:** 50-50 C13/Si28 formulation
